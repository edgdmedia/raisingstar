Our Story of Impact

An inclusive African society where every differently-abled and less privileged child has a voice and an equal opportunity.

Michael Showunmi
Founder, Raising Star Africa

Raising Star Africa Foundation was founded in 2020 by Michael Showunmi. Michael is a person with a disability from a less-privileged background in Lagos, Nigeria. Growing up with a physical disability was difficult — he could not attend nursery school because few schools made any provision for disability, and for over two years of his primary education he was carried to and from school by his friends.

Coming from a society hostile to differently-abled children, and facing societal stigma, bullying and abuse, Michael knows first-hand what it means to be deprived of education. That experience ignited his passion to inspire an inclusive African society where every differently-abled and less privileged child has a voice and equal opportunity. Since its inception, the foundation has reached over 15,000 children across communities in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To inspire an inclusive African society, where every differently-abled and less privileged child has a voice and equal opportunity.

Our Mission

To support, empower and inspire quality education and life skills among differently-abled and less privileged children across Africa.
