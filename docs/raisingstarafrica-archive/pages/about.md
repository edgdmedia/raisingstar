# About

### Our Story of Impact

Raising Star Africa Foundation is a Non-governmental organization founded in 2020 by Michael Showunmi. Michael is a person with disability and comes from a less-privileged background in Lagos Nigeria. Growing up as a child with a physical disability was difficult for him as he was not able to attend nursery school because there was no care made for disabilities in most schools. Michael was carried by his friends to and fro to school for over two years of his primary education.

Coming from a society that is hostile to differently-abled children, coupled with the societal stigma. Michael knows what it means to be deprived of education as he was a victim of bullying, hurtful teasing; threat and abuse making him miss school for days and weeks. This experience ignited and fueled his passion to inspire an inclusive African society where every differently-abled and less privileged child has a voice and equal opportunity, catering to the needs of every differently-able and less privileged child. Since its inception, the Raising Stars Africa Foundation has reached over 15,000 children in different communities across different states in Nigeria.

### Our Vision

To inspire an inclusive African society, where every differently-abled and less privileged child has a voice and equal opportunity.

### Our Mission

Our Mission is to support, empower and inspire quality education and life skills among differently-abled and less privileged children across Africa.

### Our Objectives

- To reduce vulnerability and dependency among the differently-abled and less privileged children.
- To empower over 15,000 differently-abled and less privileged with life-long skills through vocation and self-sustainability skill training by 2025.
- To advocate and give a voice to differently-abled and less privileged children through different advocacy campaigns, publicity programs, community, and social sensitization.
- To offer differently-abled and less privileged children a chance for a better life with self-reliance, food security, and improved quality of life through educational aids and scholarship.
- To promote quality learning and development among differently-abled and less privileged African children through school and classroom improvement and refurbishment.
