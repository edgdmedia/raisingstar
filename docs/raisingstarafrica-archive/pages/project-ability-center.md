# Building of the Ability Center

## About

We want to build a skill empowerment center that will cater to the education and skill empowerment of children and youth with disabilities. The Ability Center will cater to the digital and vocational skills of children and youth with disabilities, such as graphics design, website development, coding, digital marketing, bag making, catering, hair dressing, and shoe making etc. Kindly support us to build the Raising Star Africa Ability Center.
