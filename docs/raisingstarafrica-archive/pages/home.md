## About Us

Raising Star African Foundation is a Non-governmental organization founded to support, empower and inspire quality education and life skills among differently-abled and less privileged children across Africa.

### Educational Inclusion

We provide differently-abled and less privileged children with improved quality of life through educational aids and scholarship.

### Vocational Empowerment

We provide life-long skills through vocation and self-sustainability skill training to reduce vulnerability and dependency.

### Disability Awareness and Advocacy

We give a voice through advocacy campaigns, publicity programs, community, and social sensitization.
