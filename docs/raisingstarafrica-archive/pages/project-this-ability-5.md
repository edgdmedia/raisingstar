# This Ability 5.0

## About

This Ability is an awareness and advocacy event used to commemorate and celebrate children with disabilities on Children's Day. In addition, it enlightens the society about the abilities embedded in a person with disability. The theme is "Promoting Cultural Diversity among Children with Disabilities".

The event consists of a parent seminar, an art exhibition, a talent competition, and financial grants for young adults with disabilities. In the previous edition, we were joined by the U.S. Consulate virtually.

Additionally, we were joined by the Minister of Trade and Investment of Nigeria, the Commissioner of Youth and Social Development in Lagos, the Air Peace Airline Manager, the IHS Social Development team, and others. Sponsor and partner with us
