# Volunteer

Your time, skills, and resources help us carry out our mission and vision across Africa. Through them, you have an opportunity to make a significant impact on the wellbeing and education of children and differently-abled persons.

## Volunteer Form

- Full Name
- Email
- Title
- Gender: Male / Female
- Phone Number
- Location
- Employment Status: Employed / Unemployed / Self-employed / Others
- Skills/Interests:
  - Photography
  - Videography
  - Content Writing
  - Graphic Design
  - Grant Proposal Writing
  - Proof Reading
  - Digital Engagement
  - Fundraising
  - Marketing
  - Coaching and Training
  - Brand Management and Public Relation
  - Social Media Management
  - Legal Expertise
  - Girls/Women Empowerment
  - Finance and Accounting
  - Project Management
  - STEM
  - Other
- Working experience with the skills
- How did you hear about us: Our website / Facebook / Twitter / LinkedIn / Instagram / From a friend / Other
