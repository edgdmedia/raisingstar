# Renovation of Iganmu Schools

## About

Most schools for children with disabilities are dilapidated and therefore impede access to quality education for children with disabilities. We want to renovate the Iganmu Inclusive Schools to become accessible for every child, irrespective of disability. We will decorate the classrooms to stimulate learning and retention.
