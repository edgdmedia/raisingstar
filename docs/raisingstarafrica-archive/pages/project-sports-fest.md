# Sports Fest 4.0

## About

An annual sporting competition that focuses on the sporting abilities of children with disabilities. It is an event used to commemorate the International Day of Persons with Disabilities on December 3rd. Over the years, more than 1,000 students with disabilities from 30 public inclusive schools have participated in Sports Fest, which has fostered disability awareness in over 200 communities across Lagos state.

In Sport Fest 4.0, 500 students with disabilities from 10 public inclusive schools will be participating in diverse inclusive sports. In the last edition, we hosted the U.S. Consulate, the representative of His Royal Majesty – Ooni of Ife, the CEO of Landmark Event Center – Paul Onwuanibe, and the rest. This event will spotlight their abilities, confidence, grit, resilience, and determination.

The disability-friendly sporting activities that will be taking place include football, basketball, volleyball, relay races, catching the treasure, pegging the line, wheelchair racing, picking balls, table tennis, and sack races etc. Be part of our partner and sponsor.
